BTS Card Planner calculates the cheapest, most suitable type of ticket for an individual having difficulty choosing a ticket from a variety of ticket types BTS and Rabbit have to offer.

To get started, click on one of the following commuter categories:

  • Traveler: choose this option only if you are a staying in Bangkok temporarily within a short period of time.
  • Adult: choose this option only if you are an adult or any ordinary commuter who does not fall into any other categories.
  • Student: choose this option only if you are a student with a valid student ID.
  • Senior Citizen: choose this option only if you are a citizen of Thai national over 60 years of age with a valid National Identification Card.
  • Small Children: choose this option only if you are a child or student with no valid student ID.

All calculations are based on the assumption that a month consists of 4 weeks or 30 days.

This tool cannot be used with Sukhumvit Line Extension and Silom Line Extension, a series of stations which include Bang Chak, Punnawithi, Udom Suk, Bang Na, Bearing, Pho Nimit, Talat Phlu, Wutthakat and Bang Wa.
